The Refuge Website Usability Test

Fall 2016

Project kick-off

Stakeholders stated overview of organization's missions, activities, priorities, and current status

The Refuge website goals

  • To create new website
  • To increase donations
  • To increase traffic
  • To increase awareness

Heuristic Evaluation

Overall design and layout are already decent

But what can be done to improve usability and fulfillment of organization's goals?

Evaluation conducted based on Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics

Screener

Test Plan

Methodology

Test participants approx. 20-30 years old

Half students / half full-time workers

Pre-test questionnaire

Set of 5 tasks

Post-test questionnaire

Results

Summary

Task metrics calculated

Participants had no issues making donations

Or finding social media links

Minor to moderate difficulty with more specific tasks

On the whole participants were not dissatisfied with the website, stylically and functionally

Suggestions

Carry over simple, easy donation process to new website

Keep navigation simple

Pay attention to how webpages and content are organized
